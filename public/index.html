How it works

The app avoids custody and card handling

1

Collect transfer intent

The buyer chooses fiat amount, crypto asset, network, and the recipient wallet address.

2

Create provider session

The backend generates a one-time hosted checkout URL with the wallet address locked.

3

Provider handles checkout

KYC, sanctions/risk checks, card entry, Apple Pay, fees, and payment authorization happen with the on-ramp provider.

4

Track completion

Webhooks update your local order record when the provider creates, processes, completes, fails, or refunds an order.

Compliance boundary

What this website does and does not do

Included

Order form, wallet/network validation, provider session creation, redirect handling, local demo checkout, webhook endpoint, and order status page.

Not included

No direct card processing, no unlicensed money transmission, no custody wallet, no private key handling, and no internal conversion of fiat into crypto.

Production requirement

Use approved provider credentials, a public HTTPS domain, registered webhooks, and your own legal/compliance review for target jurisdictions.

Direct Crypto Pay No investment advice. No custody. Provider availability and limits vary by jurisdiction.
